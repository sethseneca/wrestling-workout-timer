# Project Brief

## Project

Wrestling Workout Timer

## Working Idea

A wrestling workout timer app for interval timing, conditioning rounds, drilling blocks, rest periods, and workout structure.

## Likely Users

- Wrestlers training alone.
- Coaches running practices.
- Parents or training partners timing sessions.
- Wrestling clubs that need repeatable conditioning intervals.

## Early Product Direction

- Fast setup for common wrestling workouts.
- Loud, readable, mat-room-friendly timer display.
- Presets for rounds, rest, drilling, live goes, stance-motion, and conditioning intervals.
- Simple controls that work during sweaty, noisy training.

## Open Decisions

- Native mobile app, web app, or responsive PWA.
- Free personal tool, paid app, or lead generator for a larger wrestling training product.
- Whether to support saved workout templates in the first version.
- Whether audio cues, voice prompts, or coach-mode controls are required for version one.

## Recommended Default

Start as a responsive web app/PWA. It is faster to build, easier to share, and can become a mobile app later if the concept proves useful.
